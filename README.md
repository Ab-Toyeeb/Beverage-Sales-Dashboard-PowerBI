# Beverage Sales Dashboard - Power BI

![Beverage Sales Dashboard](./Sales%20Dashboard.png)

## 📋 Project Overview

This is an **interactive Power BI dashboard** analyzing beverage sales performance across the United States in 2022.

The dashboard covers multiple retailers (Sodapop, BevCo, FizzySip, DreamCo), regions (Northeast, South, West, Midwest), and popular beverage brands including Coca-Cola, Diet Coke, Sprite, Fanta, Powerade, and Dasani Water.

It provides clear insights into **revenue**, **units sold**, **average price**, monthly trends, regional performance, brand contribution, and progress against monthly targets.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Insights

- Strong monthly sales growth throughout 2022
- Northeast region shows consistent high performance
- Coca-Cola and Diet Coke are the top revenue-generating brands
- Clear visual comparison against monthly revenue and quantity targets
- Retailer-wise and state-wise sales breakdown
